Shawn Rast

Developer.

I am a tinkerer obsessed with bringing crazy ideas to fruition.

I spend a fair amount of my free time maintaining and building upon the network of hacky solutions and automations I've created for myself. A series of GroupMe bots and If This Then That triggers running on a 10-year old Dell Inspiron that my parents insisted was 'obsolete' make my life run smoothly. I enjoy solving my life's problems with computers, enough that I catch myself inventing new ones just for the opportunity to write something.

I have been a musician for almost my entire life. I began studying piano when I was four, picked up a trombone when I was in the fifth grade, and now am finishing up a Bachelor of Arts in Music in addition to my degree in computer science. Programming to me is very much like performing - it's a creative outlet. I love the rush I get when I find a creative solution. Music and programming are also similar in that for each, practicing is both crucial and fun. Reading theory books alone doesn't make a good performer or programmer - a desire to practice and build skills does. I love opportunities to write new solutions, and find myself asking friends and family for ideas for tools, websites, and chatbots that would make our lives easier or more enjoyable. One of the best things about being a developer is that every day brings a new challenge and a new opportunity to build a more elegant solution than my last.

Projects

TrackRecord

Ever wish that a younger you could be your DJ for the day? That's what this project was all about. TrackRecord saves a log of everything I listen to on Spotify, allowing me to create custom playlists with the results. For example, I could save a playlist of everything I listened to at my birthday party last year, or the top 30 tracks from my first semester of college.

TrackRecord is a Node+Express web app with a MySQL database and a Python script to regularly fetch user data. It'll be live for beta soon!
